Welcome to our platform! In this guide, we'll walk you through everything you need to know to get up and running.

## Setting Up Your Account

First, create an account by clicking the **Get Started** button on our homepage. You'll need to provide your email address and choose a password.

## Creating Your First Project

Once you're logged in, head to the dashboard and click **New Project**. You can choose from a variety of templates or start from scratch.

### Choosing a Template

We offer templates for:

- **Static sites** — perfect for blogs and landing pages
- **Web apps** — full-stack applications with server-side rendering
- **APIs** — RESTful or GraphQL endpoints

## Deploying

When you're ready, hit **Deploy** and your project will be live in seconds. We handle all the infrastructure so you can focus on building.

## What's Next?

Explore our documentation to learn about advanced features like custom domains, environment variables, and team collaboration.
